You find a pile of 14 cards on the floor with words on. Although they are out of order, you eventually realise that there is one card missing.

The words are:

  • Monk
  • Religious
  • Chess
  • Djibouti
  • Bishop
  • Turkey
  • Psych
  • Bird
  • TV
  • Country
  • Raven
  • Silent
  • Knight
  • Vatican
What word should go on the missing card?

As you scrawl a word on the final card, you feel that you have done something immensely worthwhile. You feel like this final word has given purpose to your life, and that your ancestors are looking down at you and smiling.

This may be because, on the wall ahead, one of them does seem to be looking down at you and smiling. You believe his looking down on you is just because he's in an elevated position, but it's also possible he was just very condescending.
